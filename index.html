About Zeta Tau Alpha

Zeta Tau Alpha is a fraternity that promotes friendship, leadership, and service. Our mission at JMU is to raise awareness and funds for breast cancer education while building strong bonds among sisters.

Our Philanthropy

We proudly support breast cancer education and awareness through events like The Washington Commanders Crucial Catch Game and Pinkleball Tournament.
